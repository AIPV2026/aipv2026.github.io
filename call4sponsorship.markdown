## AI, Proof and Verification (AIPV)

We kindly invite companies, research organizations, and academic/industrial partners to consider supporting the 1st International Workshop on AI, Proof and Verification (AIPV).

AIPV aims to build a bridge between:
- AI / Machine Learning (including LLMs),
- Theorem proving and formal verification,
- Safety-critical and high-assurance system development.

The workshop provides a unique forum for dialogue between communities that rarely meet under one roof, fostering new collaborations and advancing trustworthy reasoning technologies.

Your support will help us:
- maintain low participation barriers,
- enable travel support for invited speakers,
- facilitate tool demonstrations and student engagement.

## Why Sponsor AIPV 2026?
- **Visibility** in a growing, high-impact research area
- Access to **talent** (students, postdocs, experienced researchers)

## How Sponsorship Helps

AIPV 2026 is designed as a **community-driven event** with a focus on collaboration over competition. Sponsorships directly support:
- Invited speaker registration/travel/accommodation,

- Organization of an official networking reception or social event to facilitate participant interaction (if applicable).
